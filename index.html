About


Who We Are


In the interest of transparency, though this page uses "We," Cold Flame Studios is currently a studio of one. I work to make accessible games for all ages, with a focus on Virtual Reality experiences. I focus on engaging students and letting them get their hands on real products slated for release. If you'd like to know more (or to hire me!), send me an email by clicking or copying the address at the bottom of this page.



What We Do


We make games with integrity and honesty. That means no BS: no loot boxes, no pay to win, no advertisements, and no underhanded practices. If something comes up that includes microtransactions, they'll be cosmetic. Our projects try to get people involved with Virtual Reality regardless of their computer experience. To keep tabs on our projects, click the Twitter icon on the top right to view our Twitter feed.

How We Do It


We're currently exclusively out-of-pocket, donation, and Patreon supported. We believe in keeping software available to anyone, and we'd like nothing more than to be able to do what we love and keep everything open and free. If you'd like to contribute by any means, click "Support" on the Nav bar at the top of the website.
